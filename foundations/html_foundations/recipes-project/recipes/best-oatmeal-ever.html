Oatmeal. My creation.

Little description from me.

Throughout my whole life, I've been eating oatmeal. Saying it gently(very darn gently), I've never been a fan of it, but things do change in life, don't they) Not that long ago, a couple of lucky times during my childhood came to my mind when the oatmeal wasn't that much disgusting. And I decided to make what I recalled. The first several attempts were not very fortunate, but I'm stubborn as hell, so I didn't give up the idea of making perfect oatmeal because, indeed, in the case of success, I'd be eating a cheap, delicious, healthy meal, which is easy to make. So, this is the time to reveal the mystery. You be ready!

Ingredients:

*And here we go*

  • Whole-grain oatmeal
  • Olive oil
  • Liquid honey(ideally the flower one)
  • Salt

That's all. Enjoy!
Just kidding. You sure need some instructions. So...

How to cook it.

Boil some water. Pour oatmeal into a bowl, add enough salt, and add a couple of olive oil tablespoons to your blessing. Then pour boiled water into the bowl so there is 1.5 times more water than the oatmeal. Put a plate onto the bowl so that the water steam is not wasted. Wait for 10 minutes. Put off the plate and add honey - pretty much enough, too. Blend the ingredients. And now, finally, enjoy your meal. Esto es incre√≠ble!
